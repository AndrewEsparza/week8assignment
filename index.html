Yellowface

Author: R.F Kuang

Description: Yellowface is a 2023 novel written by R. F. Kuang. The book was described as a satire of racial diversity in the publishing industry as well as a metafiction about social media, particularly Twitter. Yellowface is Kuang's first venture into literary fiction
